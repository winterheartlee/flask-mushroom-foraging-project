Welcome to Fungi Finders...

About

Fingi Finders was setup to create an interactive community of mushroom enthusiasts who love to explore the wilderness around them in a hunt to discover the thousands of fungal species that grow beneath our feet. The objective of the community is to unearth the various fruits of the mycelial colonies, identify them and document them on the Fingi Finders database for others to be able to explore and learn. Registered community members are able to add geographical locations along with information about mushrooms that have been found in these spots. The community is currently focused on the South Wales and West area, but the website will be expanded upon to enable people from all around the world to document their own home grown mushroom friends. So whether you're foraging with intent to eat or just want to learn more about the fantastic world of fungi, head over to our mushroom map and let the treasure hunt begin!
